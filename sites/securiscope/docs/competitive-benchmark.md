# Competitive Benchmark - BTP SecuriSCOPE

Purpose: keep BTP SecuriSCOPE visually and structurally competitive with enterprise technology websites while preserving BTP-owned messaging and factual accuracy.

## Benchmark Scope

Compare against: CrowdStrike, Palo Alto Networks, Arctic Wolf, Microsoft Security.

Use competitor and peer websites only to evaluate:

- Above-the-fold clarity and first-five-second comprehension.
- Visual hierarchy, section rhythm, contrast, and premium enterprise polish.
- Navigation clarity, page length, CTA placement, and scanning behavior.
- Service explanation depth, FAQ quality, and information architecture.
- Trust-building patterns that do not rely on invented metrics.
- Mobile layout quality, footer clarity, and contact conversion flow.

## Content Guardrail

Competitor websites are not approved sources for BTP factual copy. Do not copy competitor wording, claims, service descriptions, statistics, client names, certifications, pricing, or proof points.

Approved BTP content sources are documented in `docs/content-source-policy.md`. If a claim is not supported by BTP materials, write conservative value-focused copy instead of inventing specifics.

## Brand-Specific Comparison Focus

- Primary revenue lane: Cybersecurity strategy, protection, compliance, and resilience for enterprise environments.
- Buyer question to answer quickly: How does SecuriSCOPE help a decision maker reduce risk, save time, or improve execution?
- Visual standard: the page should feel custom-built for SecuriSCOPE, not like a generic card template with swapped text.
- Information standard: service cards should define what can be bought, who it helps, problems solved, deliverables, and next steps.

## Review Checklist

- Hero explains the service lane in under five seconds.
- Primary CTA is visible in under one second.
- Service cards are specific to SecuriSCOPE and not generic consulting language.
- Visuals support the service story instead of decorating the page.
- Process section explains how a buyer moves from inquiry to next step.
- Trust section uses methods, clarity, and delivery discipline instead of fake numbers.
- Related BTP Solutions keep visitors inside the BTP ecosystem.
- Footer logo remains visible and the footer includes useful contact paths.
- Mobile page is readable, compact, and easy to navigate.

## Update Rule

When the benchmark exposes a gap, log it first in `docs/website-audit.md`, then update the site using BTP-owned content only. After the update, run the QOS checklist and verification script from the main repo.
